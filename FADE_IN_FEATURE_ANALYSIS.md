## Core Writing & Formatting Features

- **Automatic element transitions** - Seamlessly transitions between screenplay elements as user types:
  - Scene headings (INT./EXT.)
  - Action descriptions
  - Dialogue
  - Character names
  - Parentheticals (V.O., O.S.)

- **Manual override capability** - Users can override auto-detection when needed

- Industry-standard screenplay formatting built-in and ready-to-use

- Single-keypress ability to tweak individual line lengths

### Word Processing Capabilities
- Full-featured text editing equivalent to professional word processors

- Multi-language support for international screenwriting
- Font and character formatting controls

## Screenplay Formatting Rules & Compliance

### Industry Standard Compliance
- Follows professional Hollywood screenplay format standards
- Scene numbering capability (automatic or manual)
- Proper dialogue formatting with character names centered
- Action description formatting according to industry standards
- Transition handling (CUT TO:, FADE TO:, DISSOLVE TO:, etc.)
- Parenthetical formatting for O.S. (Off-screen), V.O. (Voice-over), etc.
- Professional scene heading format (INT./EXT. LOCATION - TIME)

### Revision Colors
- **Standard Hollywood revision color scheme:**
  - White (original)
  - Blue (first revision)
  - Pink (second revision)
  - Yellow (third revision)
  - Green (fourth revision)
  - And additional colors for subsequent revisions

- Color-coded page tracking for tracking which pages were revised
- Automatic sequential color progression through multiple revision passes
- Customizable revision colors for studio-specific requirements
- Visual indicator of revision status throughout the document

### Page Management
- **Scene locking functionality** - Prevents page number shifting during revisions
- **Page locking** - Locks completed pages to maintain format integrity
- **Maintains original scene/page numbers** - Even when scenes are moved or reordered
- Critical for production and pre-production workflows where page numbers are critical
- Protects document structure during active revision phases
